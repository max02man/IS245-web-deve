Cafe

Tao has a full service cafe, with free Wi-Fi Internet service. The cafe is open from 6:30 a.m. to 11:00 p.m. Monday through Friday, and from 8:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Saturday and Sunday.

The cafe offers a variety of coffees, teas, cocoas, and fruit smoothies. In addition, the cafe offers a variety of pastries made with low-fat ingredients, natural low-calorie sweeteners, and gluten-free options.
