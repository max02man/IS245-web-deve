Healthy Eating Tips

  • Start small and aim for success -- Changing a life of habits takes time; be patient
  • Make exercise a part of your day -- It does not need to be a trip to the gym... it can be a one to two mile walk
  • Go for smaller portions -- When eating out, ask for the carry out containers and cut your portion immediately
  • Eat fresh fruits and vegetables for snacks -- Leave the candy bars behind
  • Eat only whole grains -- Whole grain breads have healthy carbs compared to bread made with bleached white flour
  • Fats are OK -- As long as they are Omega-3 and not saturated or especially trans fats
  • Add other proteins to your diet -- The proteins you eat should not be all meat; try beans and various natural nuts
  • Make sure you get enough calcium -- Various dairy products as well as certain green leafy vegetables contain calcium
  • Cut out the salt -- You get enough sodium in your diet from prepared foods; don't add more
  • Enjoy the benefits of organically grown foods -- They are free of pesticides and chemicals, and many are locally grown
